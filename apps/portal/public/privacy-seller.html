Política de Privacidade — Full Sales Seller

Last updated: 2026-07-22

Full Sales Seller is a B2B field tool for authorized sellers. This page describes data handled by the Android app (com.fullsales.seller).

Data we process

  • Account email and authentication tokens (login session)
  • Cached commerce, product, and sale records needed for offline work
  • Device network status used only to sync and show connectivity UI

How we protect data

  • API traffic uses HTTPS (TLS) in release builds
  • Tokens are stored in EncryptedSharedPreferences on device
  • Android backup of app data is disabled (allowBackup=false)

Permissions

INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE only — no contacts, camera, or location.

LGPD / data requests

Sellers act under their tenant organization. For access, correction, or deletion requests, contact your tenant administrator or email privacy@comerc.app.br.

Contact

Privacy: privacy@comerc.app.br
