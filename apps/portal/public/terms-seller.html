Termos de Uso — Full Sales Seller

Last updated: 2026-07-22

The Full Sales Seller Android app is provided for authorized sellers of a tenant organization to manage commerces, catalog, and sales in the field.

Acceptable use

  • Use only with credentials issued by your organization
  • Do not share login credentials or attempt to access other tenants
  • Offline drafts sync to the tenant API when connectivity is restored

Availability

The service depends on the tenant API and device network. Offline mode is best-effort for cached data and queued sales.
